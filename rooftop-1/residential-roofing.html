Common warning signs

Signs your home may need roofing attention

Catching these issues early can help reduce repair costs and prevent interior damage.

Interior water stains

Discoloration on ceilings or upper walls often points to hidden roof leaks.

Missing shingles

Open sections leave the roof more vulnerable to water and wind damage.

Sagging roof lines

Uneven sections can indicate structural concerns or long-term moisture problems.

Aging materials

Older roofs may still function, but deterioration can accelerate quickly.
