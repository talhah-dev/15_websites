Roofing FAQ

Questions Newark property owners ask before a roofing project begins.

These answers are here to make the early part of the process easier to navigate.

How do I know whether the roof needs repair or replacement?

That depends on the age of the roof, the spread of the damage, and whether the surrounding system still has reliable life left in it.

Do you work on commercial and multi-family buildings too?

Yes. The site includes dedicated pages for commercial roofing and multi-family roofing in addition to residential projects.

What kinds of repair issues do you handle?

Common repair calls involve leaks, weather exposure, flashing issues, missing materials, and visible signs of roof system wear.

How do I start the estimate process?

Use the contact page and share the property type, the location, and what you are noticing with the roof. That gives us a strong starting point.
