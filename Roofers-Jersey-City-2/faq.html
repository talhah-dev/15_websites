Roofing FAQ

Questions Jersey City property owners ask before starting roofing work.

These answers are here to make the first step easier, whether you are dealing with a roof issue now or planning a larger project.

How do I know if I need repair or full replacement?

That depends on the age of the roof, how widespread the damage is, and how well the surrounding system is still performing. Some roofs need targeted repair. Others are at a point where replacement gives better long-term value.

Do you work on residential and commercial properties?

Yes. The site includes dedicated service pages for residential roofing, commercial roofing, multi-family roofing, repair work, and new roofing projects.

Can you help with apartment or condo properties?

Yes. Multi-family properties often need clearer communication and more planning because the roofing work affects more than one resident or stakeholder at a time.

What kinds of repair issues do you handle?

Common calls include roof leaks, flashing issues, weather-related wear, missing materials, and visible signs that the roof system may be weakening.

Do new roofs only apply to replacement projects?

No. New roofing can fit both replacement projects and new construction or major property improvement work.

How do I get started?

Start from the contact page and share the property type, your location, and what you are seeing with the roof. That gives us a good place to begin the estimate conversation.
